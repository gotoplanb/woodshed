# Privacy Policy

**Hermit Jam** is designed to respect your privacy. Here's what you need to know:

## Data Collection

Hermit Jam does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. There are no analytics, no tracking, no ads, and no third-party SDKs.

## Apple Music

Hermit Jam uses Apple's MusicKit framework to:
- List your Apple Music playlists (for importing into setlists)
- Search the Apple Music catalog (for song lookup during playback)
- Stream or play songs via Apple's ApplicationMusicPlayer

All music data stays between your device and Apple's servers. Hermit Jam does not access, store, or transmit your listening history, library contents, or Apple Music account information beyond what is needed for playback.

## iCloud Drive

Setlist data (JSON files and tab images) is stored in your iCloud Drive container. This data:
- Syncs automatically across your devices via Apple's iCloud infrastructure
- Is visible in the Files app under "Hermit Jam"
- Is never transmitted to any server other than Apple's iCloud
- Can be edited, shared, or deleted by you at any time

## Local Storage

When iCloud is not available, setlist data is stored locally on your device in the app's Application Support directory. This data is included in device backups.

## No Server

Hermit Jam has no backend server. There are no user accounts, no cloud services beyond Apple's iCloud, and no network requests other than those made by MusicKit to Apple's servers.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, open an issue at [github.com/gotoplanb/woodshed](https://github.com/gotoplanb/woodshed).
