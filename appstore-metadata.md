## Description (4000 chars max)
Hermit Jam is a music practice companion that works with Apple Music. Import your playlists, jam through songs, and drill individual sections with loop and speed control.

**How It Works**

1. Create a playlist in Apple Music with the songs you want to practice
2. Import the playlist into Hermit Jam
3. Tap Jam to play through songs in order — like a live set
4. Tap a song to practice — see sections, loop any part, change speed

**Practice Mode**

Tap any song to see its sections (intro, verse, chorus, solo). The playhead tracks your position in the song. Tap a section to jump to it. Hit the loop button to repeat just that section over and over until you've got it down.

**Jam Mode**

Hit play to jam through your entire setlist. Songs play start to finish, one after another, with no interruption. Just like playing along with the record.

**Speed Control**

Slow down any song to 50% or 75% speed for learning difficult parts. Speed control works with locally downloaded tracks — buy the music from iTunes or import your own files, and the speed picker activates automatically.

**Edit in iCloud Drive**

Section timestamps are stored as simple JSON files in your iCloud Drive. Open them in any text editor to adjust timing, add sections, or write practice notes. Changes sync back to the app automatically.

**No Account Required**

No sign-up, no subscription, no server. Your setlist data lives in iCloud Drive. Share a setlist by sharing the JSON file.

## App Review Notes
This app requires an Apple Music subscription for streaming playback. To test:

1. Launch the app and allow Apple Music access
2. Tap the import button to see Apple Music playlists
3. Select a playlist to create a setlist
4. Tap "Jam" to play through songs in order
5. Tap a song title to enter practice mode with section looping

The app stores setlist data as JSON files in iCloud Drive. Section timestamps are edited externally (in a text editor) rather than in-app — this is intentional design, not missing functionality.
